art is therapy, movement, integration...

In Munich, a city with more than one and a half million inhabitants, we promote the integration of the hundreds of cultures that cohabit in it through dance

because music is an universal language

VOLARE

We are a group of dancers from 9 different countries living in Munich. We know how hard it is to learn the german language, understand a new culture and make friends in a different country... That is why we decided to offer FREE DANCING LESSONS as a way of providing a space in the city to socialize and make friends. Everyone can join! YOU are welcome to join! If none of us speaks your native langugage, you can still come and follow our classes. Dancing needs no words, music is universal and the most we are, the merrier. We invite you to dance the integration in Germany, our new home. Dancing needs no words, music is universal and the most we are, the merrier.

Join us to our upcomming activities!
Acrobatics outdoor

Every first and second weekend of the month, we meet in different outdoor places around Munich to practice some circus-acrobacies. Stay tuned to our Facebok site or check our calendar to could join our dance sessions.

Oriental Dances

Summer 2019 will find us learning different oriental dances. From Belly-dance to Bollywood or even Dabke!! Stay tuned to our Facebok site or check our calendar to could join our dance sessions.

Street Dances

Street dances are one of our favorites!! We meet every Friday night to throw some steps of Hip-hop, breakdance and Capoeira. Stay tuned to our Facebok site or check our calendar to could join our dance sessions.
